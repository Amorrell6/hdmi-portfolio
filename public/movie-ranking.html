The Best Movies According to Amanda

My top 3 movies of all time.

Guardians of the Galaxy

I love the characters, the action, and the comedy.

Dead Alive

This movie is ridiculous. Low budget comedy/horror with lots of over the top gore.

House of 1000 Corpses

Always a fan of Rob Zombie. Love his music and his movies. This one is a one of a kind horror film and was actually scary compared to most new horror films out now.
